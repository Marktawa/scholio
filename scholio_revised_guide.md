# Scholio Website Sections - Comprehensive Guide

## DESIGN SYSTEM
- **Colors**: Navy Blue (#1E3A8A), Black (#000000), White (#FFFFFF)
- **Font**: Arial (sans-serif)
- **Breakpoints**: Mobile (0-768px), Tablet (769-1024px), Desktop (1025px+)
- **Icons**: FontAwesome
- **Images**: Unsplash/Pexels/Pixabay
- **No animations**

---

## 1. WELCOME SECTION (PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE)

### Informational Content

**Section Title**: "A Message from Our Principal" or "Welcome from the Principal"

**Principal Information:**
- Professional headshot photo (high-quality, smiling, approachable)
- Name & Title: "Dr. John Mwamba, Principal"
- Credentials (optional): Brief mention of qualifications (e.g., "Ph.D. in Education Leadership")

**Message Content (300-400 words)**:
- **Opening**: Warm welcome to prospective families
- **School Philosophy**: Core beliefs about education and child development
- **Vision Statement**: What the school aims to achieve for students
- **Unique Value Proposition**: What sets Scholio apart from other schools
- **Personal Touch**: Brief mention of passion for education or personal commitment
- **Call to Action**: Invitation to visit the campus or contact the school
- **Signature**: Principal's signature (image or stylized text)

**Content Strategy**: 
- Write in first person ("I", "We")
- Balance professionalism with warmth and approachability
- Focus on parents' concerns: safety, academic excellence, character development
- Keep paragraphs short (3-4 sentences each) for readability
- Include 2-3 key themes: academic excellence, holistic development, community

**Example Opening**: 
"Welcome to Scholio! As Principal, it is my privilege to lead a community of dedicated educators, enthusiastic learners, and supportive families who share a common goal: nurturing the next generation of leaders, thinkers, and compassionate citizens."

### UI Design

**Layout Structure**:
- **Desktop**: Two-column layout
  - Left: Principal's photo (40% width)
  - Right: Message content (60% width)
- **Tablet**: Two-column maintained with adjusted proportions
- **Mobile**: Single column, stacked (photo on top, content below)

**Principal's Photo**:
- Professional portrait in business attire
- Rounded corners (border-radius: 10px) or subtle frame
- Shadow effect for depth
- Minimum size: 400x500px (desktop), 300x375px (mobile)
- Position: Left side, vertically centered with content

**Content Area**:
- White or light background card
- Subtle shadow or border
- Padding: 40px (desktop), 30px (tablet), 20px (mobile)

**Typography**:
- Section title: 36px (desktop), 28px (mobile), Navy Blue, Bold
- Subtitle (Principal's name): 24px, Black, Bold
- Title/credentials: 16px, Gray, Regular
- Body text: 16px-18px, Black, line-height: 1.8
- Signature: Stylized or image, below message

**Design Elements**:
- Opening quotation marks icon (large, Navy Blue, decorative)
- Signature line with principal's signature
- Background: White with subtle texture or pattern
- Optional: Decorative accent line or shape in Navy Blue

**Spacing**:
- Margin top/bottom: 80px (desktop), 60px (mobile)
- Paragraph spacing: 20px
- Photo-to-content gap: 40px (desktop)

**Image Suggestions**:
- Professional headshot with warm, welcoming expression
- Background: Blurred or neutral
- Lighting: Professional, well-lit
- Attire: Business or business casual

## 6. REVIEWS/TESTIMONIALS SECTION

### Informational Content

**Section Title**: "What Parents & Students Say" or "Hear from Our Community"
**Section Subtitle**: "Real experiences from our Scholio family"

**Review Categories**:

1. **Parent Testimonials** (4-6 reviews)
   - Focus on overall experience, teacher quality, communication, child's growth
   - Include parent name, child's grade, and years at school
   - Example: "Mrs. Jane Phiri, Parent of Grade 7 Student (5 years at Scholio)"

2. **Student Testimonials** (3-4 reviews)
   - Focus on learning experience, friendships, activities, preparation for future
   - Include student name and grade/graduating year
   - Example: "Michael Tembo, Grade 12 Student"

3. **Alumni Testimonials** (2-3 reviews)
   - Focus on how school prepared them for university/career
   - Include name, graduation year, current status
   - Example: "Dr. Alice Mwale, Class of 2015, Currently studying Medicine at University of Zambia"

**Testimonial Content Structure**:
- Photo of reviewer (if available)
- Star rating (5 stars)
- Quote (100-150 words)
- Reviewer's name and relationship to school
- Years at school or graduation year

**Sample Parent Testimonial**:
"Scholio has been a blessing for our family. My daughter has thrived both academically and socially. The teachers genuinely care about each child's success and go above and beyond. The communication from the school is excellent, and I always feel informed and involved. I've seen remarkable growth in my daughter's confidence and love for learning."

**Sample Student Testimonial**:
"I've been at Scholio since Grade 8, and it's been an amazing journey. The teachers push you to do your best but are always there to help. I've made lifelong friends and discovered my passion for science through the excellent lab facilities and inspiring teachers. Scholio has prepared me well for university."

**Sample Alumni Testimonial**:
"Scholio gave me a strong academic foundation and taught me the value of hard work and integrity. The critical thinking skills I developed there have been invaluable in my medical studies. I'm grateful for the teachers who believed in me and the opportunities the school provided."

**Additional Elements**:
- Overall satisfaction rating or Net Promoter Score
- Number of families served
- Retention rate statistics

**Content Strategy**:
